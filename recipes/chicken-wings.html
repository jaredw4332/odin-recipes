Restaurant Style Buffalo Chicken Wings

Description

The recipe begins with a blending of flour with spices, followed by coating the wings in the mixture, and then refrigerating them. While waiting, you must create the sauce which will cover the spice blend, using hot oil and a mixture of butter, hot sauce, and other spices. After 60-90 minutes, the now coated wings must be fried in oil for 10 to 15 minutes. When the timer is up, the wings must be added with the hot sauce mixture into a bowl and stirred together. Then, they may be served.

Complete instructions found below.

Ingredients

The following will yield 5 servings

  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 10 chicken wings
  • oil for deep frying
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 dash ground black pepper
  • 1 dash garlic powder

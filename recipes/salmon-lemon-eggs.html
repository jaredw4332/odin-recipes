Smoked Salmon and Lemon Scrambled Eggs

Description

Note: This recipe assumes that you have already prepared the smoked salmon and scrambled eggs.

Simply prepare a bagel and lay the salmon and eggs on top. Season, and serve with a lemon on the side.

Complete instructions found below.

Ingredients

The following will yield 1 serving

  • 1 bagel
  • a slice of smoked salmon
  • black pepper, to serve
  • a wedge of lemon, to serve
  • scrambled eggs

Directions

  1. Halve, toast, and butter a bagel.

  2. Drape a slice of smoked salmon on each half and spoon over the scrambled egg.

  3. Grind over black pepper.

  4. Serve with a wedge of lemon on the side.
